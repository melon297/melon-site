Why a job board?

At Melon 29:7, we believe you cannot build a business — or anything lasting — while a crisis is substituting itself for forward motion. For many of the people we serve, that crisis is income instability. A steady job is not a consolation prize. It is a pillar of a strong foundation — the kind of stability that makes everything else possible.
